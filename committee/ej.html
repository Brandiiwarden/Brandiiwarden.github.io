Environmental Justice

Our Mission

The Environmental Justice (EJ) Department strives towards the notion of environmental justice by educating and dismantling environmental racism, which we define as racial discrimination in environmental policy-making and the disproportionate ecological hazards that communities of color face. We aim to educate our peers about this issue and many other environmental inequities. We are committed to addressing these issues through inclusive education that is led by those most impacted, uplifting the voices of BIPOC activists and alumni. We want to create sustainable opportunities and solutions on campus for the Berkeley community, and make environmentalism a more accessible and equitable cause.
