Technology

The Tech Committee is responsible for being the Office of ASUC Senator Tellem's first point of contact with the greater Berkeley community. They curate and design the graphics and posts for Senator Tellem's social media and online presence.

Sage Pierone

Committee Chair

Sage Pierone is a sophomore studying Computer Science with a minor in Race and Law. He is from Los Angeles and is a LA Posse scholar, which is where he and Chaka first met. He hopes to work in the computer science field with a focus on race and equality, and has a passion for artificial intelligence and dismantling data bias in machine learning. In his free time, he can be found on the soccer field, skateboarding, or playing Super Smash Bros Ultimate.
