Chief of Staff

Moorea Benmosche

Chief of Staff

Moorea is a sophomore pursuing her B.A. in Urban Studies with a minor in Public Policy. Born and raised in Los Angeles, she has always enjoyed exploring cities and studying the socioeconomic implications of the design of the built environment. In the future, she aims to change the exclusive nature of urban policy to provide more resources for the populations that developers neglect. She loves airports, city views, and the number 4.

She is very passionate about making a change on campus and being involved in important decision-making regarding the student body, which is why she joined the ASUC. In her free time, she loves to play her electric guitar, and go on random spontaneous adventures at any point in the night.
